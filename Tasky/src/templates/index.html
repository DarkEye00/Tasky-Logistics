Services

Providing logistics and transportation services is our core competency.
Our main functional areas are transportation and distribution. We have enough fleet of trucks, delivery vans and equipment that are operated by highly professional persons, who are ready to cater for your transport needs. Utilizing our own fleet of trucks that range from half-ton pick-ups, 10-ton trucks, car carriers, low loaders to 40-ton semi-trailers. We provide transport to all destinations in East Africa and have the capacity to transport your cargo/goods. Precisely Tasky Logistics Ltd offers:

  • General Freight Transport: Whether it's small packages or large cargo, we offer a comprehensive solution to meet your transport needs.
  • Express Courier Services: Need something urgently? Whether same day delivery, overnight shipping, nationwide deliveries. Our express service ensures your goods arrive at their destination in record time.
  • Last-Mile Delivery: We specialize in reaching the final destination, ensuring that your products are delivered directly to your customer's doorstep.
  • Distribution: Our distribution services offer flexible, cost-effective solutions for your needs.
  • Customized transport: We offer dedicated vehicles, route optimization and special handling and secure transport.

Tasky Transport is a dynamic and innovative transport and Logistics Company, with a strategic specialized fleet, providing solutions throughout Kenya for a diverse range of customers.
